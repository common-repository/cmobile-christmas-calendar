== Description ==
CMobile Christmas Calendar is an innovative idea to market your website or blog 
your plugins & themes without losing any change. In our PRO version you get access to our 
QR platform in the cloud (https://qr.claroappstore.com)

Super easy to use, just paste the following shortcodes to your page (anywhere you want it, only one per page)
[cmob_ccholder]
[cmob_cc]


After installation, you can find configure your calendar under settings/Christmas Calendar
= Features =
* Set your destination URL for every day until Christmas
* Supports 24 and 25 days
* Nice looking countdown timer before 1: st December
* Set a "fake" date so you can test your links 
* Include "drop-down" with all previous days until today's day
* Looks wonderful on all devices, on the phone you can use your finger to open the calendar
